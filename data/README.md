# Data requirements

The complete input datasets are not included in this repository because of their size, licensing conditions, and external distribution arrangements.

## Land-use datasets

| Dataset | Year | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| HGN1990 | 1990 | Historical land-use baseline |
| LGN7 | 2012 | Intermediate land-use assessment |
| LGN2024 | 2024 | Current land-use baseline |

## Agricultural data

| Dataset | Year | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| BRP Gewaspercelen Definitief | 2024 | Agricultural parcels and crop types |
| Standard Output coefficients | 2020 | Agricultural opportunity-cost assessment |

## Population and recreation data

| Dataset | Year | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| CBS Vierkantstatistieken 100 m | 2024 | Population accessibility |
| TOP10NL Wegdeel lijn | 2024 | Walking and cycling infrastructure |
| TOP10NL Wegdeel vlak | 2024 | Recreation surfaces and potential parking areas |

## Spatial reference

All spatial analyses were performed using:

- Coordinate reference system: `EPSG:28992`
- Ecological raster resolution: `25 m`

## Reproducibility note

The notebooks document the expected filenames, processing steps, derived variables, and output locations. Users must obtain the source datasets from their respective providers and comply with the providers' licensing and redistribution requirements.

Third-party datasets are not covered by the repository's MIT software licence.
